Gaussian Process bandits for recommender tuning

Explored recommendation-system parameter tuning as a black-box optimization problem under noisy online feedback, balancing exploration and exploitation across candidate settings.

UW–Madison · atomic quantum computing software

Computational holography for neutral-atom qubit arrays

Developed computational holography tools for generating and evaluating SLM phase masks used in optical tweezer and bottle-beam experiments for neutral-atom qubit-array platforms.

  • Implemented Fourier-optics forward models, FFT-based propagation, and optimization loops.
  • Built analysis tools for trap uniformity, spot fitting, beam quality, and axial/radial diagnostics; improved array uniformity to roughly 99.5% in one workflow.
  • Worked across Python, PyTorch, Julia, SciPy, slmsuite, FourierSLM calibration, and experimental camera-image workflows.
